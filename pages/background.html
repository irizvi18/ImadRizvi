Background

Outside of my academic interests, I also have a passion for learning languages (currently studying Portuguese and Dutch), soccer (huge EPL and Manchester City fan), and standup comedy (ranging from Kumail Nanjiani to Trevor Noah to Anthony Jeselnik).
