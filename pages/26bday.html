THE CHALLENGES

The weekend will feature a variety of challenges, a subset of which will take place. On the first day of 2026 I set myself goals in 13 different categories. The challenges will involve each of these categories

  • Reading. Book challenge (will specify later).
  • Cooking. Cocktail making contest.
  • Athletics. Tug of war.
  • Comedy. We will all sit in a room and each person can specify one video to be played. Anyone to laugh or even remotely smile / move their face is eliminated.
  • Sports. We will play soccer.
  • Soccer. FIFA tourney.
  • Spiritual. There will be a box of words/phrases. If you get someone else to say it, you get the point and can pick a new one. Each person can submit one phrase.

  • Movies. Everyone will suggest a movie to watch anonymously and the choices will be voted on / ranked collectively.

  • Engineering. Best thing engineered wins, interpret how you will.
  • Work. Trading Game.

Other challenges will include jeopardy, a board game, guess the Imad, etc.
