  • 07/30/2014: (Friendly) Mancity 2(1)-2(2) Liverpool @ Yankee Stadium. Liverpool won on penalty kicks.
  • 03/16/2019: (UCL) Barcelona 5-1 Lyon @ Camp Nou.
  • 03/16/2019: (UCL) Manchester City 7-0 Schalke @ Etihad Stadium.
  • 07/07/2019: (FIFA Women's WC Final) USWNT 2-0 Netherlands @ Groupama Stadium (Lyon).
  • 04/11/2023: (UCL) Manchester City 3-0 Bayern Munich @ Etihad Stadium. This was the season we ended up winning the Champions League and the highlight was Rodri's goal and also getting to see Haaland live the first time.
  • 06/26/2024: (Copa America Group Stage) Argentina 1-0 Chile @ Metlife Stadium
  • 07/05/2025: (FIFA Club World Cup QF) Real Madrid 3-2 Borussia Dortmund @ Metlife Stadium. Got to see an Mbappe scissor kick to seal the game in added time.
  • 12/06/2025: (EPL) Manchester City 3-0 Sunderland @ Etihad Stadium. Sunderland away fans came marching into the stadium chanting "Nordi, Nordi, Nordi. Used to play with Lionel Messi. But he said he was shite, so he jumped on a flight. Now he's playing for Reggie Le Bris" (to the tune of the Entertainer). Ruben Dias rocket, Josko Gvardiol header, and this Rayan Cherki assist. We were in row 3 and so close to the touchline; half the fans in our section were trying to talk to the "line-o" the whole match.
  • 12/09/2025: (League 1) Leyton Orient 1-1 Luton Town @ BetWright Stadium. Great atmosphere though the football quality was painful to watch after seeing Mancity 3 days earlier. The wanker point and chants were great to see. Was cool to see Jack Wilshire on the touchline (Luton Town manager). Thought Tayo Adaramola (Leyton Orient) stood out as the best player on the pitch and after the match found out he's a Crystal Palace loanee which makes sense.
